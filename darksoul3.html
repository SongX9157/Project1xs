Dark Soul III

Overview Sekiro Zelda Dark Soul Monster Hunter Introduction

Description

Dark Soul III is a new action RPG game set in the world full of dark fantasy elements. Exciting dungeons, fierce monster fighting and breakthrough online elements make up a truly unique RPG experience. Dark Soul breaks the barrier with seamless world setting, motivates players to explore the game world and create unique game experience. When players encounter powerful enemies and explore new places, it will bring great sense of achievement.


Equipment

Players are equipped with a variety of weapons to fight with the enemy, such as bows, swords, knives, spears and magic wands. Shields can be used as auxiliary weapons, but they are mainly used to divert enemy attacks and protect players from damage. Each weapon has two basic types of attack, one is standard attack, and the other can enhance attack power. Although the enhance attack will consume more stamina, it will deal a larger damage.


Boss

There are a large number of boss in the game, and they all have their own characteristics. Black soul 3 has designed several stages of features for almost every boss. When entering a new stage, the previous moves and attack rhythm will change greatly. In this way, the ability of players to adapt to the circumstances is particularly important.


Scene

The scenery of lothric Kingdom and its surrounding areas is very visual impact, which is an unprecedented exquisite design of from studio. The scene map is not only full of amazing architectural miracles and detailed decorations, but also contains rich interactive elements. Those make people indulge in the delicacy and reality of the map.
