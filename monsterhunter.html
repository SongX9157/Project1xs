FEATURES

katana

The combination of attack range, attack frequency and the cool skills makes it popular among the players. But it is hard to use for those new players. Also, it has the special set called the Spirit Meter mechanic. This allows hunters to build up the energy, which can then be transferred into the weapon by unleashing a specific combo.

light bowgun

With excellent mobility, various kinds of bullets and various props, light bowgun players are often plays a important support role in the game. Light bowgun can use special bullet to heal the teammate, stun the monster and also explode the monster with extreme high damage. The versatility of this kind of weapon made it popular.

bow

Bow is a little similar to the weapon light bowgun. It also has the high mobility, and several kinds of special arrows with different function. But the special arrow is more limited for using and the bow can deal more damage than light bowgun in common.

lance

Lance is the weapon with highest defence and tenacity over the whole game. When hold the shield up, the lancer can defence almost all the attack with little damage taken. And after taking the damage, it can deal conterattack with high damage to the monster.

palico

Palicoes are the best partner and the reliable comrades when hunting the monsters. It is specialized in multiple abilities include healing, attacking, defencing and supporting.
